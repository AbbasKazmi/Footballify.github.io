Football Round-Up 5/22/22

Bedlam In The Prem

Championship drama was dramatic with the title, relegation and european places all up for grabs. Manchester City were crowned champions after a comeback From 2-0 down to win 3-2 against Aston Villa. Matty Cash scored in the first half with Coutinho putting in a second before Man City scored 3 goals within 6 minutes to win the game. Liverpool were 3-1 Victors over Wolverhampton Wanderers but it was all in vain. In other news, Tottenham needed a win to secure top 4 and they did so by thrashing norwich 5-0. Arsenal also scored 5 in a 5-1 win against Everton but they only managed to qualify for the Europa League. Manchester United after a dissapointing 1-0 loss to Crystal Palace on also qualified for the Europa League. Now looking at the bottom, Burnley lost and Leeds won meaning Leeds stay up and Burnley are relegated to the Championship. Insane Drama.

Serie A Title Goes to Milan

The Serie A Title Race Ended this past weekend as AC Milan and Inter Milan both picked up victories Meaning that AC Milan have won the Scudetto. Inter Finish in 2nd place qualifying for Champions League alongside Napoli and Juventus who finished 3rd and 4th respectively. Lazio and Roma claimed the Europa league spots in 5th and 6th. Fiorentina qualified for the Conference League. The sides relegated are Cagliari, Genoa and Venezia with Salernitana making a miraculous escape from relegation.

La Liga Ends In Shock Results

Real Madrid already confirmed the La Liga title but that did not stop us from some entertaining games. The standout perhaps is Barcelona's 2-0 Loss to the hands of Villareal which really did not affect the standings too much. Barca finished second behind Real Madrid with Atletico Madrid and Sevilla claiming the other 2 Champions League Spots. Real Betis and Real Sociedad qualified for the Europa League and Villareal, who beat Barcelona, qualified for the Conference League. At the other end, Granada, Levante and Alaves will be heading down to the 2nd division.
